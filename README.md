I'm a **Full-Stack Web Developer** with a strong frontend focus, passionate about crafting interfaces that are as functional as they are beautiful. I've completed internships at **Syntecxhub**, **DevelopersHub Corporation**, and **Apexcify Technologies** — and I'm currently building my freelance career, helping startups and small businesses establish strong digital presences.
